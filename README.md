# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 13, 2026**

Khayal Sharifli ("we", "our", or "us") built the **Kids Math** app as a free, ad-supported application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

### Information Collected Automatically

**Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics)**
We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, including:
- App opens and session duration
- Screens viewed
- Device type, operating system version, and language settings
- General location (country/region level only)
- App crashes and performance data

This data is collected in aggregate and cannot be used to personally identify any individual user.

**Advertising Data (Google AdMob)**
Our app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect:
- Device advertising identifier
- IP address
- General device information

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), ads are served in a non-personalized manner.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- We do **not** require account creation or login
- We do **not** collect photos, contacts, or location data
- We do **not** store any user data on external servers
- All app preferences (dark mode, language, progress) are stored **locally on your device only**

## Children's Privacy

Kids Math is designed for children aged 5-10. We take children's privacy very seriously.

- We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable regulations
- We do **not** knowingly collect personal information from children
- All ads displayed are child-appropriate and non-personalized
- Our app is tagged as "Designed for Families" and complies with Google Play's Families Policy
- No behavioral advertising is served to children
- The app does not include any social features, chat, or user-generated content

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Google AdMob | Displaying advertisements | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Google Firebase Analytics | Anonymous usage analytics | https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy |

## Data Storage

All user preferences and progress data are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore. This includes:
- Dark mode preference
- Language selection
- Quiz progress and completed levels
- Gift points balance

This data is **never** transmitted to any server. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data.

## Ad Choices

Our app displays:
- **Banner ads** at the bottom of screens
- **Rewarded ads** that users can optionally watch to earn in-app rewards (gift points)

Rewarded ads are always opt-in and never forced. All ads are served through Google AdMob and comply with Google's ad content policies for family-friendly apps.

## Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on servers. Anonymous analytics data collected by Firebase is retained according to Google's standard retention policies.

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Delete local data** by clearing the app's data in your device settings or uninstalling the app
- **Opt out of personalized ads** through your device's advertising settings
- **Reset your advertising identifier** through your device settings

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
